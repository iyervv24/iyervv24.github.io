Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together

What types of projects do you take on?

I focus on strategic enterprise technology initiatives, particularly ERP implementations, WMS optimizations, and IT transformation programs. I work with organizations ranging from mid-market companies to large enterprises across various industries.

Do you work remotely or require on-site presence?

I adapt to project needs. Many strategic planning and consulting phases can be done remotely, while implementation phases often benefit from on-site presence. I'm experienced with hybrid approaches that maximize efficiency and effectiveness.

What's your typical engagement model?

Engagements vary based on needs - from strategic consulting and assessments to full program leadership. I offer project-based work, interim executive roles, and ongoing advisory relationships. Each engagement is tailored to your specific requirements.

How do you approach change management?

Change management is integral to every project. I focus on stakeholder engagement, communication planning, training programs, and cultural transformation alongside technical implementation to ensure sustainable adoption and success.

What industries do you have experience with?

Over 35 years, I've worked across manufacturing, retail, distribution, healthcare, financial services, logistics, and energy sectors. This cross-industry experience provides valuable perspective on best practices and common challenges.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, I believe in long-term relationships and sustainable success. I offer various post-implementation support options including optimization reviews, performance assessments, and ongoing advisory services to ensure continued value realization.
